Welcome to my travel page. Here you'll find a list of places around the world that I've been. Enjoy:)

Mozambique, Africa, 2005

This was my first trip to Africa. I had wanted to go to Africa ever since I was young--I'd say The Lion King influenced me. I went with a church on a mission trip. Mozambique was unlike any other place I had ever been before. The extreme poverty is what impacted me the most.

Cape Town, South Africa, 2008

This was my second trip to Africa. Overall prettier I'd say than Mozambique. Beautiful mountains and good beaches. This was also a mission trip with my church.

Cape Town, South Africa (Part II), 2009

I went back with my church to the same part of Cape Town as I did in 2008 where we ministered to the poor in a living community called a Township.

Scotland & England, 2009

From Cape Town I flew to Scotland and met up with a friend. We then toured Scotland and England.

Mozambique, Part II, 2012

This was my last trip to Africa as of the present day. We partnered with a Brazilian pastor and his team to minister to the natives in the beginning stages of their church plant.
